      ACM Conferences
      ACM and ACM Special Interest Groups (SIGs) sponsor more than 170 computing conferences, workshops, and symposia around the world. These events, which range in size from conferences with tens of thousands of attendees to small workshops, mirror the state-of-the-art in their respective fields and attract renowned experts from a broad range of computing disciplines, who submit leading-edge research papers for presentation at the conference and inclusion in the published proceedings. Many of these conferences have earned preeminent status in their respective fields, attracting attendees from around the globe.
